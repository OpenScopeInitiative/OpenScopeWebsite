Who We Are

OpenScope is a student-run science communication platform dedicated to making scientific knowledge accessible, engaging, and student-driven. We feature work by high school students across diverse scientific disciplines, with a primary focus on written articles.

  • Encourage young writers and thinkers to explore science and build public portfolios
  • Engage the general public, especially students, with digestible and creative science communication
  • Emphasize clarity, curiosity, and interdisciplinary thinking

OpenScope is made up of 140+ high schoolers across 12+ countries.
